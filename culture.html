Afghan Cultural Heritage

Afghanistan’s traditional clothing is a window into its rich history, regional diversity, and timeless craftsmanship. Each pattern, color, and embroidery tells a unique story.

Herati Attire

Originating from Herat, these dresses feature intricate embroidery and vibrant colors. They are often worn during celebrations and cultural events.
